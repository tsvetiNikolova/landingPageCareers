Who We Are

More than a tech company

At Diadraw, we believe that meaningful work starts with meaningful connections. We're not just a place that delivers IT services. We’re a space where people with shared values come together to exchange ideas, grow professionally and support each other as individuals.

For us, success isn’t measured only in completed projects. It’s reflected in the relationships we build, the trust we nurture, and the constant pursuit of becoming better -as a team and as people.

Our Way of Working

Hybrid flexibility, with a preference for shared presence

We offer hybrid roles, but we encourage being together in the office. Not because we don’t trust people to work remotely - but because we believe that presence fosters creativity, dialogue, and deeper collaboration. For us, the office is a space to connect, not control.

What You’ll Find Here

  1. A supportive and respectful work environment
  2. A team that values openness, trust and collaboration
  3. Informal activities and initiatives that strengthen bonds and creativity
  4. Opportunities to participate in decision-making and planning
  5. Room to speak up, try new things and grow in your own direction
